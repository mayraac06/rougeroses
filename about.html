About Us

Welcome to Rouge Roses where we share your passion for roses. Like you, the team at Rouge Roses is made of flower enthusiasts that love to encapsulate special events. That's why we seek out the best stories and fresh roses that last forever available today. Every roses you select, we hope to capture your special moment.

The Rouge Roses Website launched in December 2020 with the belief that sharing roses, helps show your loved ones you care about them.

The Rouge Roses management team is well equipped to serve people's needs and vision regarding rose arrangement needs. Our team has experience in the flower market and retail fields.
